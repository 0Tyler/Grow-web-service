Competency Evaluation in GROW

(This is description of questionnaire) Each competency was selected from hundreds of competencies and skills reported in the social sciences literature on the basis of several criteria: 1) representation across different countries and cultures; 2) correlation to success in relevant job functions (as verified by executive search firms); and 3) assessable by peers.
